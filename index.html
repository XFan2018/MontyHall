Monty Hall Game

There are three doors in front of you. Behind one door is a car. Behind the others are goats. You win if you pick the car, otherwise you lose. After you pick one door, the system will open a door without the car, and you will have a chance to switch the choice. Will you have a higher chance to win if you switch? Monty Hall problem
